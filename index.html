About Me

Hi there! My name is Yaakov Yisrael and I am a training in Columbia Engineering Boot Camp to become a Full Stack Developer. I was always a very deep thinker, curious, paying attention to details and asking questions. I love technology and prior to entering high school, I built my own custom desktop computer from scratch. In high school, I started a company “Ramson Tech”, through which my partner and I built and sold custom computers.

Over the years, my passion for technology evolved from hardware more towards software development. I also have a deep passion for languages (I speak fluent English, Hebrew, and Farsi and started learning Arabic and some Chinese), which in-part, drives me to learn programming. I am fascinated by the field of software development and hope to immerse myself in it upon graduating.

Fun fact- I like to compose my own songs on the piano, which I’ve been playing for the past 20 years. I have superb hand-eye coordination, which makes me a fast typer, both on the piano and behind the computer ;)
